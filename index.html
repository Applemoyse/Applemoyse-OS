LUNA


A fluffy, meowing Discord companion!

On 17 April 2023, LUNA, the in-house cat bot and adorable mascot was created with 10 commands ready to be used. As a newly born bot, she has plenty of room to grow and there are many future plans for her. This cute kitty was inspired by the developer's real-life feline companion, Luna.
